remove_mat

PURPOSE

Sets the mat field of the OBJNAME of OBJTYPE to empty.

SYNOPSIS

function [subj] = remove_mat(subj,objtype,objname)

DESCRIPTION 

 Sets the mat field of the OBJNAME of OBJTYPE to empty.
